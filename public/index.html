Roohani Talaba Jamaat

Roohani Talaba Jamaat (RTJ) was formed keeping in view its young members moral, spiritual and educational development. RTJ compromises of students from schools, colleges and universities. Its members are encouraged to host educational activities and competitions etc. Its student members manage Roohani Talaba Jamaat. This grants them the opportunity to practice and enhance their management skills.

The mission of RTJ is to work together and perform the best role possible in being a Muslim. It is a structured, organized platform that motivates its youngsters in gaining an education. These platform facilities guides and council its students on studying options that are available for them. Alongside assisting in choosing the field of study and its scope (to progress and scale). It grants awareness and helps its students develop a successful business, improve life skills, better health management, and explore scientific inventions, all through a systematic teamwork procedure. It is a well organized and modern spiritually connected setup that brings success both in this world and the hereafter.

PATRON IN CHIEF

Sajjan Saeen, real name (Khawaja) Muhammad Tahir Bakhshi Naqshbandi. He was born on March 21st 1963, in District Larkana, Pakistan. He belongs to the Mujaddidi branch of the Naqshbandi Sufi order. His spiritual lineage goes back to the Holy Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) through Sheikh Ahmad Sirhindi, the Mujjadid of the eleventh Hijri century.

The message and teachings of Sajjan Sain are straightforward to understand. He says that one should consider their actions and exert effort in bettering oneself. One should not object not to speak ill of any other being, religion or sect. He teaches to work for the prosperity of Islam, other beings and bettering one's character. One should not use their power or resources to hurt others; instead, they should be like a river, silently benefitting others. He teaches and insists on practising the remembrance of God in one's heart (Zikr Qalbi). This method helps one strengthen their bond with Allah the Almighty. He often advises following the practice (Sunnah) of the Holy Prophet (PBUH) as well as encouraging his students to recite and understand the Holy Quran, which will, in turn, allow them to love all the creation of Allah and His Holy Prophet's (PBUH) happiness. His emphasis is to purify one's soul and to achieve closeness to Almighty Allah.

6 Core Agendas

The Six core agendas reflects the love of Hazoor Sajjan Sain for all humanity. He is directing and preparing us for the future, which indeed is a blessing for all of us.

HEALTH

If you are not in good health, how will you support your family or achieve your dreams? Meditation, daily exercise, healthy diet, consulting professionals for medical assistance & walking daily are essential. Under this core agenda, free medical camps are organized. Awareness campaigns such as the importance of wearing a helmet are also practices promoted by this agenda.

Education

Education is an essential component of an Islamic society. Providing educational awareness, financial aids, educational co-curricular activities, career counselling events, workshops for children and women's education, formation of educational institutes, SAT test and exam guidance programs etc. are held considering this core agenda.

Business

“Business” is the Sunnah of our Holy Prophet (PBUH). In business you grow significantly which isn't possible without a job and financial literacy is essential to come out of the “rat race”. Helping in taking start-ups, conducting business guidance events etc. all come under this agenda. Many RTJ members have already become successful businessman.

Social Welfare

Social welfare is concerned with welfare and well-being of the poor, vulnerable and marginalized segments of society. This excluded population has fewer opportunities for economic, social, and human development, and remains caught in a vicious cycle of poverty; therefore, economic growth needs to be equitable for improving the living standards of all strata of the society.

Environment Tolerance

Contributing toward a welfare state by reforming ourselves and making our environment better comes up with environmental tolerance. Forgiving others, cultivating positive thoughts, obeying laws, cleaning the environment, plantation, spending time with our family, especially our parents, having a good character and patience within us all come under environmental tolerance.

Use Of Scientific Education And Inventions

Stepping forward in the field of science and technology to adopt and use them. Researching in science, cultivating positive aspects of tech, using electronic media especially social media platforms wisely for positive purposes comes under this element. We have to give our contribution in this field and respect those who have already contributed. We should use technology to make own life easier.

ROOHANI TALABA JAMMAT

Roohani Talaba Jamaat founded on 5th October 1975. Today, its branches are found all around Pakistan. Its head office is located at Allahabad Sharif, Kandiyaro. The students associated with this organization abide by the Shariah of the Holy Prophet (PBUH). Its members include students of the school, colleges and universities.
