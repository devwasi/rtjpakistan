What is RTJ?

The Roohani Talaba Jamaat (R.T.J.) formed, keeping in view the young members’ moral, spiritual and educational development. The R.T.J. comprises of school, colleges and universities students. The members are encouraged to host educational activities, activities, competitions etc., that develops management, teamwork and other essential life skills in them.

The R.T.J. mission is to work together and become the best practicing Muslims and a role model for others. It is a well-organized platform that motivates youngsters to get ahead in education, scientific development and 6 core agendas. This platform facilitates, guides and counsels on each aspect of life, especially education and thinking. It gives awareness and helps in successive business, life skills, health maintenance and scientific inventions through adequately systematic teamwork procedures. It is a well-organized and modernized spiritually connected setup that brings success in this world and the life hereafter.

Foundation

Roohani Talaba Jamaat was founded on 05th Oct. 1975. With head office at Allah Abad Sharif Kandyaro today, it has branches all around Pakistan. The associated members comply with Sharia principles strictly. Students of the school, colleges and universities are members of this organization.

A notable aspect of the members is their transformed lifestyle. The basics of Islam and Sunnah are of great importance for them, along with modern education. Those who were once disobedient are respectful. Roohani Talaba Jamaat has provided them with a platform to walk in a positive direction.

Mission & Vision

Behind every creation, there is a purpose. Likewise, every organized struggle has a unique goal. Today many organizations are working for Islam, each with a different goal and objective.

The R.T.J. aims to guide Youth and assist them in Education, Scientific Inventions, Health, Business, Good life skills and Spirituality to create members perfect for the flags of leadership. It has a complete hierarchical Structure, i.e. Central Body, Provincial Bodies, District Bodies, and Branches.

The Purposes and Objectives of RTJ

  • To create true love of Al-Mighty Allah and His Prophet Hazrat Muhammad (PBUH) in students’ hearts.
  • To promote Islamic Life Styles in all walks of life of a student.
  • To promote Islamic Teachings in Muslims, especially among students.
  • To create the Love of Zikar Allah and to create enthusiasm to spend time with people of good deeds.
  • To create eagerness and awareness of scientific and technical education within the students.
  • To make every possible effort to solve the educational problems of students.
  • To integrate the spirit of good character within the students.
  • To create a practical and analytical approach for the solidarity of Pakistan and the unity of the Islamic World.
  • To create qualities of individual its teamwork, writing and debate.
  • To create the spirit of social work.
  • To arrange spiritual meetings as well as of Zikar Allah.
  • To establish the branches of R.T.J. in Pakistan and in abroad as well.
  • To communicate with entire Centers, Reformative and Welfare institutions of countries of the World for Religious
    • services.

Thus RTJ aims developing in youth Self-introspection, love for humanity, piety, remembrance by Heart, meditations and Pious gathering and awakening attributes likewise, instructive and reformation, Shariat and Tariqat, Love and fraternity, Spirituality and truthfulness, trust and responsibility and Positive thought. Those who Possess High Potential and should be the Pride of the Nation.
